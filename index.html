Baku White City

This test project shows the interactive map of Baku White City on the base of some buildings. It was made on a new technology that is based on game engines. It is shown on the video the top view of some buildings and use it with the help of touchscreen. It’s possible to rotate the building too. In the second part of the video there is an interactive walk in the small side.

The Last DeadEnd

The Last DeadEnd is a 3d action game with elements of survival horror. The game is expected to support first person and third person modes. The action of the game will take place in Icherisheher (old city) area of Baku, Azerbaijan. The main character of the game is a scientist studying ancient Zoroastrian book “Avesta”. He discovers that he is assigned to a mysterious mission to fight with evil forces.

Under Occupation: Shusha

Under Occuption – the first official 3d computer game in Azerbaijan. The game takes place in near future, after provocation by the enemy, Azerbaijan army takes action to liberate the lands that are under occupation. The main actions are taken place in Shusha city. The main goal of player is to liberate the city from the enemy. The game was created in FPS genre and was developed within two years.
